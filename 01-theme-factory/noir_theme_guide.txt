
BLOOD ASSASSIN - MINIMALIST NOIR THEME GUIDE
============================================

COLOR PALETTE
-------------
Primary:   #0A0A0A (Near Black)
Secondary: #F5F5F5 (Off White)
Accent:    #C80000 (Deep Red - use sparingly)
Support:   #808080 (Mid Grey)
           #282828 (Dark Grey)

TYPOGRAPHY
----------
Font Family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif
Title Size: 72pt (no bold)
Heading: 36pt
Body: 24pt
Caption: 14-18pt

DESIGN PRINCIPLES
-----------------
1. CONTRAST: Only high contrast combinations
   - Black on white
   - White on black
   - Grey for secondary elements

2. MINIMALISM: 
   - Maximum 3 elements per slide
   - Single focal point
   - Extensive negative space

3. TYPOGRAPHY AS DESIGN:
   - Text is the primary visual element
   - Size creates hierarchy
   - No decorative fonts

4. COLOR RESTRAINT:
   - 95% black/white/grey
   - Red accent for critical emphasis only
   - No gradients or shadows

5. LAYOUT:
   - Center-aligned for impact
   - Left-aligned for reading
   - Asymmetric for tension

6. IMAGERY:
   - High contrast black & white only
   - Silhouettes preferred
   - Minimal detail

MOOD & TONE
-----------
- Stark
- Mysterious  
- Elegant
- Dangerous
- Sophisticated

WHAT TO AVOID
-------------
- Ornamental elements
- Multiple fonts
- Bright colors
- Complex layouts
- Photorealistic images
- Gradients/shadows
- Rounded corners
- Decorative borders

APPLICATION NOTES
-----------------
This noir theme strips away everything except essence.
Every element must justify its presence.
Space is as important as content.
Less is always more.
