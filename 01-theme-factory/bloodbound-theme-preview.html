Chapter 13: Shadowed Alliances
The Blood Vault
"When crimson meets silver under twilight's veil,
The Bloodbound shall rise to tip fate's scale."

Elara Nightshade stood at the threshold of the Crimson Court, her dual nature pulsing beneath her skin like liquid twilight. The ancient prophecy echoed in her mind, each word carved in golden fire across her consciousness.

Seraphiel's wings cast shadows that seemed to breathe with their own life, while Valeria's duplicitous smile promised both salvation and betrayal. In Civitas Noctis, trust was a luxury none could afford, yet it was the only currency that might save them from the Nightbringer's awakening.
